Our Care Services

Comprehensive, personalized care designed around each individual — delivered with warmth, skill, and unwavering respect.

What We Provide

Care That Meets You Where You Are

At Mercy of God, we understand that every person's needs are different. That's why we offer a range of care services — from full-time residential living to flexible homecare support — all built around the individual, not a one-size-fits-all template.

Whether your loved one needs around-the-clock supervision or just a little extra help during the day, we're here with the right level of support at the right time.

Licensed, trained staff
24/7 availability
Family-centered approach

Detailed Service Descriptions

Core Service

Adult Residential Facility

A licensed, 24-hour supervised residential home where adults receive comprehensive personal care, meals, and community support in a safe, home-like setting.

Our Adult Residential Facility is designed for individuals who need consistent daily assistance but deserve more than an institutional environment. We keep our home small and personal because we believe quality care requires genuine relationships — not just routines.

  • 24-hour supervision and personal care assistance
  • Three nutritious, home-cooked meals daily plus snacks
  • Medication management and health monitoring
  • Housekeeping, laundry, and home maintenance
  • Structured social activities and community engagement
  • Safe, accessible, home-like living environment
Best for: Adults who need daily living assistance in a structured, supervised home environment with around-the-clock care.
Core Service

Homecare Services

Personalized in-home care delivered in the comfort of the client's own home — flexible, respectful, and tailored to individual needs.

Some people thrive best in their own space. Our homecare services bring professional, compassionate care directly to the client, helping them maintain independence while getting the support they need. Whether it's a few hours a day or more comprehensive coverage, we adapt to your schedule.

  • Bathing, grooming, and personal hygiene assistance
  • Dressing and mobility support
  • Meal preparation and nutritional support
  • Medication reminders and health monitoring
  • Light housekeeping and errand assistance
  • Companionship and emotional support
Best for: Individuals who prefer to remain in their own home but need regular assistance with daily tasks, health monitoring, or companionship.
Core Service

Board & Care Services

Full room, board, and personal care in a warm, home-like environment designed to promote comfort, belonging, and well-being.

Board and care is more than a place to stay — it's a place to live. Residents receive nutritious meals, clean and comfortable living spaces, and personal care support while being part of a small, close-knit community. We create routines that bring structure without feeling institutional.

  • Private or shared accommodations in a home setting
  • Three daily meals and snacks, accommodating dietary needs
  • Personal care and hygiene assistance
  • Housekeeping, laundry, and linen services
  • Recreational and social activities
  • Transportation coordination for appointments
Best for: Adults who need a supportive living arrangement with full room and board plus personal care, in a community-oriented home.
Support Service

Daily Living Assistance

Hands-on help with the everyday activities that matter most — eating, bathing, dressing, mobility, and more — delivered with patience and dignity.

Activities of daily living (ADLs) are the foundation of personal independence. When these become challenging, our caregivers step in with skilled, gentle assistance that preserves autonomy wherever possible. We help people do as much as they can on their own and provide support where it's truly needed.

  • Bathing and personal hygiene
  • Dressing and grooming
  • Eating and feeding assistance
  • Mobility, transfers, and walking support
  • Toileting and incontinence care
  • Cognitive prompting and daily routine guidance
Best for: Individuals who need hands-on help with personal care tasks while maintaining as much independence as possible.
Support Service

Personalized Care Plans

Every resident receives an individualized care plan built around their unique needs, preferences, routines, and goals — and updated as those needs change.

Cookie-cutter care doesn't work. That's why we invest time upfront to understand each person: their health needs, their daily routines, their likes and dislikes, and their personal goals. We build a care plan that reflects who they are — not just what they need — and we revisit it regularly to make sure it stays relevant.

  • Comprehensive intake assessment and family consultation
  • Individualized care goals and daily routines
  • Dietary preferences and nutritional planning
  • Coordination with physicians and specialists
  • Regular care plan reviews and family updates
  • Flexible adjustments as needs evolve
Best for: Every person in our care. Personalized plans are the foundation of everything we do — no exceptions.
Support Service

Safe & Supportive Housing

A clean, accessible, well-maintained home environment designed from the ground up for safety, comfort, and dignity.

A safe home is the foundation of quality care. Our facility is designed with accessibility and safety as top priorities — from grab bars and non-slip surfaces to adequate lighting and emergency systems. But safety doesn't mean sterile. Our home is warm, inviting, and designed to feel like home because it is one.

  • Fully accessible, barrier-free living spaces
  • Safety features: grab bars, non-slip floors, emergency call systems
  • Comfortable common areas for socializing and activities
  • Well-lit hallways, bedrooms, and outdoor spaces
  • Regular maintenance and cleanliness standards
  • Fire safety and emergency preparedness systems
Best for: All residents. Safe housing is a guarantee — not a service tier. Every person in our care benefits from these standards.

Additional Support Services

Every service below is available to our residents as part of their care — because complete support means covering every need.

Medication Management

Careful tracking of medication schedules, refills, and coordination with pharmacies and physicians.

Respite Care

Getting Started

How It Works

From your first call to move-in day, we make the process simple, transparent, and stress-free.

1

Reach Out

Call us or fill out our contact form. We'll listen to your situation, answer your questions, and schedule a time to meet.

2

Tour & Assessment

Visit our home, meet our team, and see the environment firsthand. We'll conduct a care assessment to understand your loved one's needs.

3

4

Your loved one moves in and our team begins providing compassionate, around-the-clock care from day one. We stay in close contact with your family.
