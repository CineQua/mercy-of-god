Our Story

Born from Faith,
Built with Love

Mercy of God began with a simple, profound belief: that caring for others is one of life's most sacred callings. What started as one family's commitment to helping a loved one has grown into a licensed, trusted Adult Residential Facility and Homecare service.

Our founders experienced firsthand the challenges families face when searching for quality care — the worry, the uncertainty, the longing for a place that would treat their loved one with the same tenderness they would. They couldn't find what they were looking for, so they built it themselves.

Today, Mercy of God is still family-owned and family-operated. That's not just a label — it's a promise. It means every decision we make is guided by the same question: "Would this be good enough for our own family?" If the answer isn't yes, we do better.

Over the past decade, we've had the honor of serving hundreds of families across the community, and each experience has deepened our commitment to doing this work with excellence, humility, and love.

What Drives Us

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, dignified, and person-centered residential care and homecare services in a safe, nurturing environment — treating every individual with the unconditional love and respect they deserve, regardless of age, ability, or circumstance.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted name in adult residential care in our community — a place where families find peace of mind, residents find belonging, and caregivers find purpose. We envision a world where every person in need of care is met with grace and dignity.

Our Promise

We promise to care for your loved one the way we would care for our own. We promise to communicate openly, act with integrity, and never compromise on safety or quality. Your trust is something we earn every single day.

“Whatever you did for one of the least of these brothers and sisters of mine, you did for me.”

— Matthew 25:40
What We Stand For

Our Core Values

These aren't just words on a wall — they guide every decision, every interaction, every moment of care we provide.

Compassion

Compassion is at the heart of everything we do. We see the person first — their story, their feelings, their needs. We don't just perform tasks; we care with genuine warmth and empathy.

Dignity

Every person under our care has inherent worth. We honor their identity, their preferences, and their right to live with respect. Dignity isn't something we give — it's something we protect.

Family

We treat every resident like a member of our own family. That means going the extra mile, showing patience on hard days, and celebrating the good ones. We build real, lasting relationships.

Integrity

We do the right thing — even when no one is watching. We communicate honestly, keep our promises, and hold ourselves accountable. Families trust us because we've earned it.

Faith

Our work is guided by a deep faith that every life has purpose and value. This doesn't mean we impose beliefs — it means we serve with a sense of calling, grace, and spiritual grounding.

Safety

The safety of our residents is non-negotiable. From medication management to facility maintenance, we maintain the highest standards of care and compliance — no shortcuts, no exceptions.

The People Behind the Care

Carefully Selected

02
